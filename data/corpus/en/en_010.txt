Biotechnology applies biological systems and organisms to develop products and technologies. Genetic engineering modifies DNA to produce crops with improved yield and disease resistance. CRISPR technology allows precise editing of genes, opening possibilities for treating genetic disorders. Bioinformatics uses computational tools to analyze large biological datasets including genomes and protein structures. Pharmaceutical biotechnology develops biologic drugs like antibodies and vaccines. Stem cell research explores regenerative medicine and tissue engineering. Industrial biotechnology uses enzymes and microorganisms for sustainable manufacturing processes.
