Robotics engineering combines mechanical design, electronics, and software to create machines that interact with the physical world. Industrial robots automate repetitive tasks in factories with high precision and speed. Collaborative robots work alongside humans safely, adapting to changing environments. Autonomous drones use sensors and navigation algorithms for delivery and surveillance. Medical robots assist surgeons with delicate procedures, improving accuracy and reducing recovery time. Soft robotics uses flexible materials inspired by biological organisms to handle fragile objects. Swarm robotics coordinates multiple simple robots to accomplish complex tasks together.
