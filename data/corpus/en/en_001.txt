Artificial intelligence is transforming the world of technology and business. Machine learning algorithms enable computers to learn from data and make predictions. Deep learning, a subset of machine learning, uses neural networks with multiple layers to process complex patterns. Natural language processing allows machines to understand and generate human language. Computer vision enables systems to interpret and analyze visual information from the world.

AI applications range from virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa to autonomous vehicles navigating city streets. Neural networks mimic the human brain's structure, processing information through interconnected nodes. Reinforcement learning trains agents through trial and error, rewarding desired behaviors. Supervised learning uses labeled datasets to teach models, while unsupervised learning finds hidden patterns in unlabeled data. Transfer learning applies knowledge from one domain to another related domain. Generative AI creates new content, including images, text, and music. AI ethics concerns bias, fairness, transparency, and accountability in automated systems. Explainable AI aims to make AI decisions understandable to humans. Edge AI brings processing closer to data sources, reducing latency and bandwidth usage.
