Renewable energy sources are transforming how electricity is generated and distributed worldwide. Solar panels convert sunlight directly into electricity using photovoltaic cells. Wind turbines capture kinetic energy from moving air to generate power. Hydroelectric dams harness the potential energy of flowing water. Geothermal energy taps into heat from the earth interior to produce steam and drive turbines. Energy storage systems including batteries and pumped hydro ensure reliable supply when generation fluctuates. Green hydrogen produced from renewable electricity offers a clean fuel for transportation and industry.
